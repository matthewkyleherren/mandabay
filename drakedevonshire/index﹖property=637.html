Your County Getaway Awaits

  • Recognized as a Trip Advisor 2025 Travelers' Choice Award Winner
  • Conde Nast Readers Choice 2024 Award Winner

Nestled in Prince Edward County, Drake Devonshire is a lakeside retreat where contemporary design and creature comforts meet a bucolic, natural setting. Enjoy our new sauna, lakeside chaise lounges, boules and pickleball courts, plus an inspiring art walk—each designed to elevate your stay. Thoughtfully designed rooms blend modern comfort with timeless beauty, while locally inspired dining and captivating art bring the region’s culture to life. Lounge by the lake, explore nearby vineyards, distilleries, cideries, breweries, and galleries, or discover the charm of surrounding villages. Every detail is crafted to balance relaxation and rejuvenation.
