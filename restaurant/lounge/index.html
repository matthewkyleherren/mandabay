The Lounge Is Our Feature Restaurant + Bar

The Drake Lounge is the heartbeat of our iconic hotel—a place where locals and visitors gather to enjoy expertly crafted cocktails, shareable plates, and some of the neighbourhood’s best sushi. By day, our living room–meets–dining room finds its own rhythm—a stylish, casual setting perfect for shared work, brunch, drinks, or meals with friends. It’s a welcoming spot where the comings and goings of the neighborhood meet the relaxed elegance of a hotel lounge. The menu runs from brunch through late night—approachable yet full of flavor—offering dishes designed to satisfy every craving. As the sun sets, the lounge shifts into an amber glow — dimly lit, with a classic downtown atmosphere. Plush leather booths and carefully curated tunes set the early evening vibe just right. Whether you’re here for a casual drink, a meal with friends, or something a little more special, our main stage strikes the perfect balance between relaxed and refined. As late night approaches, the energy subtly builds — the DJ takes over, spinning a seamless blend of beats that turn the lounge into a vibrant after-dark scene. It's where conversations deepen, cocktails keep flowing, and the rhythm carries you through the night. At the Drake Lounge, food, drink, and the city’s creative pulse come together in a seamless experience—one that feels effortlessly authentic and unmistakably rooted in the spirit of The Drake.
