Toronto / Prince Edward County

Located in the heart of Toronto and Prince Edward County, Drake Hotel Properties are boutique hotels that blend culture, community, and hospitality for curious travellers who like to keep things interesting. The Drake is where creativity is sparked by connection inviting you to explore, engage, and be inspired by the singular experiences that define each of our neighbourhood hotels.

About us

Explore Our Locations

Explore Our Locations

From Torontos vibrant urban core to the scenic charm of Prince Edward County, The Drakes properties are embedded in storied, character-rich neighbourhoods offering a one-of-a-kind escape that feels both rooted and refreshing.

  • Toronto

    Toronto

    In the heartbeat of Toronto just steps from major attractions, venues, and the energy of the downtown core The Drake stands as a beacon of creativity and culture. Located on dynamic Queen Street West, the hotel brings together art, music, and culinary experiences to offer something for every kind of traveller, from business guests mixing work with play to weekenders soaking up the city's electric energy.

    Explore
    Explore

  • Prince Edward County

    Prince Edward County

    Set along the picturesque shores of Lake Ontario in bucolic Prince Edward County, Drake Devonshire is a refined retreat where art, design, food + wine, and hospitality intersect. With sweeping waterfront views, locally inspired cuisine, and thoughtfully curated on-property and local experiences, it's a destination for vacationers, wedding celebrators, locals, and guests blending business with downtime all drawn to unwind, connect, and find creative inspiration.
